Our Story

The three of us met this summer at the 2018 Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, that teaches and encourages young girls to code and enter the future tech field. The program also hopes to raise the percentage of women in this field and end the gender gap. During this program we are taught to code, and each week we learned a new and different language such as Python, Arduino C, HTML, CSS, and Java. Throughout the last 2 weeks, we were given a challenge of making a final project where we would use all the new skills we have learned and applying them to make a website, game, app, and so much more.

Out of all the projects and topics we created a website about makeup. You might be asking yourself why makeup? And what will make it different from other makeup websites? Our mission was to create a unique web page to try to change stereotypes in the makeup and beauty community. Living in a society where women are often being shamed for wearing too much makeup and often stereotyped into "trying to look good or impress the males", we are trying to change that mindset to allow individuals to express themselves. Makeup is not about impressing others, it is more about expressing who you are and it allows others to show their creativity. It is an art and should be appreciated. To carry out this goal, we decided to create DVE as a makeup website that not only has makeup and skincare products, but also a place where people of all colors and genders to upload an image of themselves on the website and draw "makeup" on the picture if they do not have access to the colors, shades, and specific types of products. We also have quotes throughout the web page to allow individuals to see daily reminds to boost their self-esteem and make them feel good.
