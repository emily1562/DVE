Our Story

All 3 of us met each other this summer at the 2018 Girls WHo Code's 7 week long Summer Immersion Program that teaches and encourages young girls to code and enter the tech field in the future, hoping to raise the percentage of women in this field. During this program, we are taught to code, each week learning a new and different language such as Python, Arduino C, HTML, CSS, and Java. During the last 2 weeks, we are presented with the challenge of making a final project where we would use all the new skills we learn and apply them to make a website, game, app, and so many more.

Out of all the projects and topics we can create and make, why makeup? What will make it different from other makeup websites> What makes DVE different from other makeup websites is our mission to try to make a change in the community of makeup. Living in a society where women are often being shamed for wearing too much makeup and often sterotyped into "trying to look good or impress the males", we are trying to change that mindset. Makeup is not about impresisng others, it is more about expressing who we are as people and it allows others to show their creativity and who they are. It is an art and should be appreciated. To carry out this goal, we decided to create DVE as a makeup website that not only has makeup and skincare products, but also a place where people of all colors and genders to upload an image of thier on the website and draw the "makeup" on the picture if they do not have access to the colors, shades, and specific types of products.
